Oatmeal Breakfast

Description

Oatmeal is a popular and healthy breakfast all over the world.

In this recipe, you will learn how to cook it in a tasty yet simple way.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Nuts
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Add a bit of butter to a saucepan and cook it until it's liquid.
  2. Add the oatmeal and cook it until is toasted.
  3. Add the milk and cook it all for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Finally, serve it into a bowl and add bits of nuts and a sliced banana!
  5. Additionaly, you can mix it with some cinnamon.
