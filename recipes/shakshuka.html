Shakshuka

Description

The shakshuka is a simple yet tasty African dish that consist only of four basic ingredients:

  • Crushed Tomato
  • Eggs
  • Yogurt
  • Spices

It's a highly customizable dish where you can be creative and add some other ingredients that you might find delicious.

Ingredients

  • An Onion
  • Crushed Tomato
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Yogurts
  • Parsley
  • Cumin
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • A Red Pepper
  • A Green Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the peppers in tiny cubes along the mushrooms and the onion.
  2. Brown the onion on a pan
  3. Add the mushrooms and the peppers to the pan and cook them for 30 seconds or less.
  4. Add the crushed tomato and all the species except the parsley and cook it all with low fire.
  5. When the tomato has some consistency, add the eggs and cover the pan until they are done.
  6. Finally, add some persley from above and enjoy the dish with some toasts!
